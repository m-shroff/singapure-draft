Singapure is provided to local users at a more affordable price than existing brands. It improves access to reliable and affordable drinking water for the local population, adhering to ESG and SDG commitments.

Singapure water is a highly purified mineral water, sourced from deep in the ground near Mount Batukaru. The water is cleaned in our factory in Kerambitan, Tabanan using a four-step purification process.

The following processes are used to purify our water:

  1. Reverse Osmosis
  2. Ozonation
  3. Ultrafiltration
  4. Ultraviolet purification

For your peace of mind, the water is also tested in our in-house laboratory two times a day to insure that it is clean, something that is rare in the industry.
