Our Water

Hydrate happier. Water worthy of you.

Soft, smooth and light on the palate, our water is easy to drink, and very refreshing. The perfectly balanced pH of the water gives a consistent taste experience. It responds beautifully to carbonation – in fact, the process makes the water taste even better – which is perfect for those who prefer bubbles in their water

Why Singapure?

Truly pure.

Our water is cleaned using a four-step purification process with no chemicals at the factory in Tabanan making it extremely hygienic. An on-site laboratory tests the water twice a day to ensure the purity of every gallon that leaves our premises. We store the filled gallons inside a covered warehouse to preserve the quality of water from the sun and other elements. All because we care about our water and we care about you

Well paired.

Our water perfectly complements any meal, because it has a balanced and neutral flavour which will not change the taste of your food between sips. This is why Singapure is the preferred choice of many restaurants and cafes.

Beautiful brew.

Coffee tastes smoother and has more depth of flavour when made with our water. This is because TDS (total dissolved solids) in the water is the ideal range for perfect extraction, as recommended by an international body of specialty coffee manufacturer.

Healthful and healing.

Our water has a balanced pH level of 7.2 and can be easily digested by everyone.
